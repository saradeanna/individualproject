Hello everyone! My name is Sara Deanna Natasha Binti Sophian, and I am 20 years old, born on February 6th, 2004, at Damai Service Hospital in Kuala Lumpur. Throughout my life, I have faced many challenges and opportunities that have shaped my character and goals. My educational journey has been varied and filled with different experiences that have helped me grow.

I began my education at Q-Dees, where I attended kindergarten at the age of three. This early exposure to learning sparked my interest in education, and I continued my studies at SK Jalan 3 Bandar Baru Bangi from Year 1 to Year 4. My time there was enjoyable, as I made many friends and appreciated the supportive environment.

However, in October 2014, my family faced a difficult situation. I moved to Ipoh, Perak, to live with my grandmother while my mother was unwell due to thyroid issues. This change was necessary because my father needed to take care of my mother, and I was grateful for my grandmother's support during this tough time.

Eventually, we moved to Bandar Seri Putra, where I completed Year 6 at SK Bandar Seri Putra. Adjusting to a new school was not easy, but I adapted and continued to do well in my studies. I then attended SMK Bandar Seri Putra, where I studied from Form 1 to Form 5. During high school, I joined a KAA class in Forms 1 to 3, which included extra subjects like Bahasa Arab. This experience expanded my knowledge and helped me appreciate different cultures. In Forms 4 and 5, I focused on Additional Mathematics, Accounting, and Business, which I found particularly interesting and rewarding. These subjects not only challenged me but also shaped my future academic path. 

Achievements:
- Completed Additional Mathematics with distinction.
- Joined KAA classes for advanced Arabic.
- Adapted well to various school transitions.
